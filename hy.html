Introduction

AUPATELECOM (PTY) LTD is a leading ICT company that specializes in Network Contracting and Maintenance services. Founded in 2012 by Mr. Aupa Makhobotlela and Mr. Donald Pule, both accomplished professionals with extensive experience in the South African Telecom Industry. The company is proud to be 100% Black owned, contributing to the empowerment and growth of the local community.

Our Services

  • Power: Rectifier Installations & Upgrades, Solar Power, Battery Upgrade, and Epoxy Installation.
  • RF: RF Maintenance, DAS & In-building Installations, BTS Cabinet Installations.
  • Security: Site Alarm Installations, CCTV Installations, Access Control.

Our Team

Our success is attributed to our exceptional management team. Mr. Aupa Makhobotlela: A seasoned expert in Radio Planning and Optimization with a combined 5 years of experience at MTN and Vodacom. Mr. Donald Pule: An expert in Radio Maintenance, Network Infrastructure, Energy projects, and Budgeting, with 16 years of experience at Vodacom SA. Both directors' expertise has fueled AUPATELECOM's growth from 5 employees to 60+ young professionals.
